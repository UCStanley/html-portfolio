My All-Time Top 3 Movies

King of Boys

An eye-opener movie that exposes the criminal underworld of power in Lagos, Nigeria.
Directed by Kemi A.

Jagun Jagun

This awesome epic movie is a perfect display of the Yoruba ancient culture and tradition. A must-watch.
Directed by Adebayo Femi

Black Book

This action movie beams its light on the corruption in the Nigeria Police Force and how an ex-soldier fought gallantly to expose them.
Directed by RMD
